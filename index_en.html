porCausa

Journalism & Research

Get involved

We promote journalism & research on poverty and social inequalities in collaboration with media, researchers and social organizations

porCausa supports public service, data-driven journalism based on multidisciplinary teams, multimedia, and agile development

We cover the whole journalistic process: research, in collaboration with social researchers; data gathering and analysis, and distribution through media agreements and online tools and products

We are an independent, non-profit organization. We are funded by private donations. We are commited to open data and open source software development
